About us

After 10 successful years with SSSS Cafe, S4 TEAs now expands the supply chain to an E Platform. Now, our buddies can order online the finest tea and brew at home like connoisseurs. We began our journey as Tea Brewers in S4 TEAs, and now we are extending our hands to provide our customers with the same fine Tea Experience in the comfort of their homes. Mr. Sandesh Kale, whose inspiration was his loving father, Mr. Nana Kale (Sir), a renowned tea maker and taster himself, leads the way!

Being the first tea room in southern India, S4 TEAs began with just 15 varieties of tea, and now it brews over 150+ varieties of tea from around the world! Every leaf is handpicked from the organic gardens by farmers practicing the Biodynamic cultivation method. S4 TEAs sources tea directly from farmers worldwide with utmost love! Sandesh emphasizes that it's love that adds taste to his tea. Every tea served at S4 TEAs is freshly brewed by world-class tea masters!

100% Natural

Array of Tea at S4 TEAs are sourced directly from the farmers and finest tea producers in Nilgiris, Darjeeling, Assam. Our herbal teas are sourced from Egypt, Germany and Australia.

Quality Product

Quality and Servers is the major motto of S4 TEAs, Most of the teas at S4 are picked from the organic farm and Bio Dynamic Cultivation practicing farmers.
